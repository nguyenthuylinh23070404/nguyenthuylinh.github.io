Fruit List

Apple

A delicious fruit with a crisp texture and sweet-tart flavor.

Banana

Known for its soft texture and sweet flavor, bananas are rich in potassium.

Mango

Mangoes are juicy, tropical fruits that are loved for their sweet and tangy taste.

Strawberry

Strawberries are bright red, juicy, and have a sweet yet slightly tart taste.

Watermelon

A refreshing fruit, watermelon is known for its high water content and sweet taste.
